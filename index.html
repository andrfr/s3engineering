Sustainable Software Systems Engineering

Sustainability

As designers of software technology, we are responsible for the long-term consequences of our designs.

for Software Systems

Software is the backbone of our society. We need to understand and manage its impact from a sustainability perspective.

by Software Systems

We develop concepts and solutions to improve and analyze sustainability in various domains.

Sustainability for and by Software Systems

The necessary effort integrate sustainability considerations into everyday business often seems too high. Concrete concepts and tools are missing.

In our research, we address this challenge from two angles: (1) How to improve sustainability for software systems? (2) How to improve sustainability by software systems?
