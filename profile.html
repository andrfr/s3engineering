Why Sustainable Software Systems Engineering?

Sustainability - the capacity to endure - is a major challenge for modern society. The ubiquity of software systems makes them both, a part of the problem, but also a potential solution. Concrete concepts to integrate sustainability considerations into everyday business are still missing. And so, the effort to effectively tackle this challenge often seems to high.

Our Research Areas

We address the challenge of sustainability from two angles. First, we believe that sustainability is not sufficiently integrated into traditional software development approaches. To improve "Sustainability for Software Systems", we develop extensions to existing approaches as well as new concepts. Second, we explore the potential of innovative software systems to directly address sustainability issues - this is what we call "Sustainability by Software Systems".
